# The Laughing Maw

**Tags:** #location/town #fight-pit #marked

Sunken stone arena behind the Caravan Quarter. Cheers echo like the Maw itself is laughing.

## Description (read-aloud)
> Blood, sweat, and salt spray the sand. Marked fighters with glowing palms trade blows with Wilds beasts and desperate challengers. Betting is ferocious.

## Features
- Fights every night at dusk
- Marked get free entry + 10% of purse
- House takes 30% (run by a council of senior Marked)

## Rumors (d6)
1. “Win three in a row and the Maw itself will call your name.”
2. “Losers sometimes get dragged across the Span — never seen again.”
3. “The pit floor is paved with aethersteel shards.”

## Secrets (DM only)
> [!secret]
> - The council sells the worst losers to Nyx as “raw material.”
> - One of the regular beasts is a polymorphed former champion.

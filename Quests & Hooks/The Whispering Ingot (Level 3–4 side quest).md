# The Whispering Ingot (Level 3–4 side quest)

**Given by:** “Honest” Omar in the aftermath of the Wildlings attack  
**Reward:** 200 gp in salt crystals + the Ingot itself  
**Hook:** Omar palms the humming ingot to the party — “Solve it before the Tithe claims it. 50 gp now, double when you crack it.”

# Ma Jessa Carver
**Race:** Human (variant)  
**Class/Level:** Fighter (Champion) 6 (retired)  
**Role/Alias:** Proprietor of the Broken Span Inn, “Ma”  
**Alignment:** Neutral Good  
**Status:** Alive

**Appearance & Voice**  
Broad-shouldered woman in her late 50s, throat a rope of old scars, iron-grey hair in a tight bun. One of the first Marked — her palm sigil now barely glows. Voice like gravel dragged across steel.

**Manner & Goals**  
Maternal to newcomers, keeps the peace with an iron fist in a velvet glove. Secretly protects every 30-day fugitive who earns her respect.

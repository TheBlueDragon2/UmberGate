# Captain Renza Thorne

**Tags:** #npc/town #span-wardens #leader

Human female, early 40s. Tall, scarred, left eye a patch of etched aethersteel (functional but creepy). Carries a salt-forged halberd named “Last March.”

**Personality:** Duty above all. Fair, but zero patience for chaos.  
**Quote:** “Thirty days is mercy. Day thirty-one is mine.”

**Secrets (DM only)**
> [!secret]
> - Her eye was taken by one of Nyx’s early “perfect” subjects.
> - Quietly recruiting anyone who’s been inside the Maw — planning a pre-emptive strike if the war reaches the Span.

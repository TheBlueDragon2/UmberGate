# “Taxman” Korr

**Tags:** #npc/town #iron-tithe #ogre

Ogre male, 9½ ft, surprisingly well-spoken. Wears a ledger chained to his belt like a holy book.

**Personality:** Bureaucratic, polite, absolutely merciless.  
**Quote:** “Ten percent. Plus pain-and-suffering fee if you make me stand up.”

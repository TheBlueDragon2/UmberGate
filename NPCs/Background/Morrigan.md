# Morrigan

**Tags:** #npc/background #mysterious #fey-touched #associated-with-wyatt

Striking woman, enigmatic and unpredictable. Claims a deserted island as her own. Provides mysterious gifts and aid to Wyatt Tindall.

**Personality:** Smirking, knowing, appears when needed but seldom.

**Voice:** Soft, whispering, with a wry smile.

**Abilities:** Conjures items from thin air, teleports others, provides potions and incantations that grant magical abilities (e.g., spectral hand, familiar).

**Quote:** "Until next time."

**Secrets (DM only)**
> [!secret]
> - True nature tied to the Wilds' chaotic magic; symbol resonates with Vane's sigils.
> - Companion trinkets escalate near the Maw, bringing Wilds-influenced items.

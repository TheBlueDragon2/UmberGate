# Iron Jack

**Tags:** #npc/background #enemy #mutineer #associated-with-wyatt

Former first mate of Wyatt Tindall. Led mutiny on the Dreadful Shadow, turning it into a pirate ship.

**Appearance:** Rugged sailor, iron hook hand (or similar prosthetic), scarred from battles.

**Personality:** Charismatic poisoner of minds, ruthless, deal-maker.

**Location:** Hiding in Umbergate's Dustveil shantytown, gambling at the Laughing Maw to raise coin for Wilds escape. On day 5/30 of sanctuary.

**Quote:** "The ship was always meant for greater things than your games."
